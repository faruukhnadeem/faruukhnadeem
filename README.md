## About

I am **Faruukh Nadeem**—full name **Hafiz Muhammad Faruukh Nadeem**—an educational leader and institution builder whose professional career began in 2007.

My experience includes school and college management, institution building, teacher development, multi-school administration, parenting education, professional-skills education, community service, orphan support, social welfare, and international administration.

My educational work is rooted in **Dhudray Sharqi, District Gujrat, Punjab, Pakistan**, while my professional experience also includes the United Arab Emirates and Mexico.

## Current Leadership

- **Chief Administrator** — Danish International Grammar School
- **Founder** — Canadian Advanced Skills Academy
- Pursuing an **M.Phil in Educational Leadership and Management** at the University of Management and Technology, Lahore

## Selected Experience

- Began professional work in computer and English-language education in 2007
- Led academies and a girls college before establishing institutions in rural District Gujrat
- Began construction of an educational institution in Dhudray Sharqi in 2011; classes commenced in 2013
- Managed **15 privately managed government schools**
- Served as **Managing Director** of Noor Alsabah Information Technology in Dubai, UAE
- Developed educational partnerships, teacher-development initiatives, and community-focused programs
